SWRL rules

  • Rule 1
  • Rule 2
  • Rule 3

Rule 1back to ToC or SWRL ToC

Infers the manufacturer reporting an incident.

involves device op(?inc,?dev) , has manufacturer op(?dev,?man) -> reported by op(?inc,?man)

Rule 2back to ToC or SWRL ToC

Infers the device affected by a corrective action.

triggered by op(?act,?inc) , involves device op(?inc,?dev) -> affects device op(?act,?dev)

Rule 3back to ToC or SWRL ToC

Infers ImplantableActiveDevice when isImplantable and isActive are both true.

Medical Device c(?dev) , is implantable dp(?dev,"true"^^boolean) , is active dp(?dev,"true"^^boolean) -> Implantable Active Device c(?dev)
